Y Vote

Our Mission:

Our goal is to raise voter turnout in the United States, which has some of the lowest voter turnout percentages of any developed country.

Why vote?

Voting is a fundamental process in the democratic system. Without strong voting turnout, our government does not function as intended.

With every election since the 1990, voter turnout has been steadily decreasing in the United States while other developed countries' turnouts have been increasing.

Every year, less and less people are having their opinion heard and taken into consideration by the leaders of the United States.

It is up to you, and everyone that can vote, to change this reality and cast your vote.
